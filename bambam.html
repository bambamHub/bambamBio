Biography

Bambam Gupta is an Indian Sofware Engineer who is the creator and principal developer of MineCraft.He also created the many apps and game. He was born on August 30, 2003 in Ara,Bihar. He developed the game MineCraft as a student project in 2024. MineCraft has become one of the most widely used game in the world.
